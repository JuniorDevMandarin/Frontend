About Minicoders

Why should kids learn coding?
Coding is, truly, one of the simplest ways for children to boost their problem-solving skills, while also developing the ability to bounce back after failure. Our video tutorials and Virtual Assistant will make their learning journey as smooth as possible.
Can kids learn how to code while playing?
Of course, do you know the Montessori System, a famous game-based school technique that uses the play as a learning method? In the same way we use the Digital-Metaverse environment to teach kids the basic principles of Coding and Programming while they play an adventure game inside Roblox.
For which age range is Minicoders best suited?
Hope, inclusiveness, respect, friendship and Play-to-Learn. If your kids can turn on a tablet, a computer or a phone they’re ready to start with Minicoders. We like to say the core ages are between 7 and 12 years old.
Is minicoders available in other languages?
Yes! As of now, you can access Minicoders in English, Spanish, Portuguese and Italian and many more languages in the future.
What about the difficulty of the tasks?
The difficulty of the tasks is enough fo kids 6-12 to require effort, but not hard enough to be impossible for them. Younger kids will find the educational videos in the apps and the virtual assistant very helpful when stuck in a logic challenge.

About suscriptions

How do I link my Parental Area at minicoders.com with Roblox game experiences?
In order to access our game experiences, you must first enter your children’s Roblox ID in your parental area at minicoders.com. After entering the children RobloxID please request access to our private Roblox group: https://www.roblox.com/groups/12929013/minicoders21
Once your request to the private group is accepted (mind it can take up to 8h) all our educational Roblox experiences will be open to play by your Roblox ID. Our experiences have a “Minicoders Parental Area” button to link with your parental area at minicoders.com. Link happens when you enter the minicoders code you will find in your Parental Area. Once this is done, you will be able to follow all of your children achievements!
How do I purchase a subscription?
In order to purchase a subscription just have to click on any “Start now” or “Subscribe” button, choose the plan that fits you best and follow the instructions. You can upgrade or cancel at any moment.
How many children may I register per account?
Up to 3 kids per account. Extending the Parental Area to 2 or 3 kids is just +20%
How often will I be billed?
That depends on the subscription plan you choose. It could be every three months or yearly. We also offer a lifetime subscription, which means only one payment for unlimited lifetime access.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription you just have to access your profile preferences / subscription / cancel, and follow the instructions
Do you have a satisfaction guarantee?
Yes we do! We’d love for you to continue your learning journey with us, but if you believe our platform is not a great fit for your little one, we offer a 30 day money-back guarantee. In order to make use of it, please contact us at client-support@minicoders.com

About Technical Questions & Security

What does Minicoders do to protect safety and privacy?
Roblox is COPPA and FTC approved, meaning it is a company that cares a lot about child safety across all of their experiences. We could not find a better metaverse platform where to locate our play-to-learn experiences made for kids.
Does Minicoders collect any private data on my child?
Minicoders collects no personal information about the players except for their nicknames, avatars, and learning progress on Roblox. You can read more about our Privacy Policy here.
Does Minicoders allow any communication between players?
Communication among players cannot happen in the Minicoders Kids Apps. In our game experiences in Roblox, communication can happen under the Roblox Privacy policy.
Does the app work on a tablet?
Yes, our Minicoders Kids apps are available for iOS and Android devices. Roblox clients are also available for iOS and Android devices, as well as Windows PCs and Macs.
